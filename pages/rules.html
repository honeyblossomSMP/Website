HoneyBlossomSMP Rules

Server Rules

Welcome — please read these rules so everyone can enjoy the server.

  1. Use common sense and act respectfully toward other players.
  2. Treat others with respect; harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language is prohibited.
  3. If asked to verify your age by staff, you must comply; refusal may result in removal.
  4. Play fairly — do not use exploits, dupes, hacked clients, or cracked accounts.
  5. Claims are protected; staff may also enforce a buffer zone around claims when necessary.
  6. PvP is optional and high-risk; enabling PvP is at your own risk. Do not kill players who have PvP disabled.
  7. Keep public chat appropriate. Avoid excessive swearing, sexual content, gore, political debate, or other topics that make others uncomfortable.
  8. Public chat must be in English to keep moderation consistent.
  9. Do not share personal information (yours or others').
  10. Disallowed software includes X-ray, autoclickers, KillAura, Baritone, and other cheating tools.
  11. Avoid lag-inducing builds (zero-tick farms, excessive redstone or structures that harm server performance).
  12. Do not TP-trap, TP-bait, use others' homes/warps without permission, or harass players to teleport to you.
  13. Follow staff instructions, provide evidence when reporting issues, and do not argue punishments in public chat.
  14. Do not submit false reports.
  15. Rules may change — staff decisions and updates take precedence; check Discord for the latest rules.

If you have questions about these rules, open a support ticket in Discord and a staff member will assist you.
